The Most Excellent Pie!

Recipe from King Arthur Flour King Arthur Flour

A delicious mix of berries, cherries, and apples, topped with sweet and crunchy streusel.

ingredients

filling

  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 1 tablespoon Instant ClearJel®
  • 3 cups fresh or frozen raspberries or blackberries or strawberries (a mixture is great!)
  • 3 cups peeled diced apples
  • 1 cup dried cherries
  • 2 tablespoons cherry concentrate

topping

  • 1 1/2 cups old-fashioned rolled oats or quick-cooking oats
  • 1 cup light brown sugar
  • 3/4 cup King Arthur® Unbleached All-Purpose Flour
  • 3/4 teaspoon cinnamon, Vietnamese preferred
  • 7 tablespoons melted butter

directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F.
  2. Blend the sugar and Instant ClearJel®. Add the fruit and cherry concentrate and stir until combined.
  3. Divide among four 2-cup baking dishes. There will be about 1 1/2 cups filling per dish. Set aside.
  4. To make the topping, combine the oats, sugar, flour, and cinnamon. Pour in the butter — mix till the mixture is crumbly.
  5. Divide the topping among the four dishes of berry filling.
  6. Bake for 30 to 35 minutes for fresh berries, 40 to 45 minutes for frozen berries, until the topping is lightly browned and the filling is bubbly.
  7. Remove from the oven, and cool at least 15 minutes before serving. Top with ice cream or whipped cream, if desired.
